## Descritpion of App
- In this project 2 mobiles are included
- One is say yours, second is say your mom
- Your mom mobile has this app
- You gave her call, she did not lift
- After some minimum number of times you call her, this is what happens
- Your mom app will take a photo using back camera
- The photo will be uploaded to a website
- And the app will send a sms to you with the photo url
### Prerequisite: 
- They need to open the app
- Why? Android wont allow opening of camera when app is in background
